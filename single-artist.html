Gopi Mohan

Fusion Folk Maestro

Gopi Mohan is a visionary artist who seamlessly weaves the intricate melodies of classical Carnatic music with the pulsating energy of modern electronica and folk rhythms. Hailing from Kochi, Kerala, his music transcends borders, finding a unique space that is both deeply rooted in Indian tradition and globally contemporary.
